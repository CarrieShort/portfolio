About

I'm a full-stack JavaScript developer with a history of design. I've been working in web design and development for ten years. I got my start in 2004 at a Spectral Fusion, a non-profit focused on teaching students to build websites and then have them apply their skills to design and build websites for on campus organizations and departments. There I learned how to manage a project, design and build websites from the ground up using HTML and CSS. As the years progressed I acquired more experience in design and development improving my library of tools. I've always preferred the coding side of things and recently enrolled in Code Fellows to improve my JavaScript skills as well as acquire a better understanding of backend programming.
